About Me

FRONT-END DEVELOPERDESIGNER & DEVELOPER

WHAT DESGIN MEANS TO ME?The way I view design is as a form of art and expression, also that it is something complimented by an artistic interest. By having a strong artistic passion and using it in combination with learning the skills and technology of design allows you to become a truly gifted designer. Good design should reflect inspiration, when you can take the inspiration of what a person wants to create you can manifest that into your own development, in what will become a quality finished product(design).

WHAT CODING MEANS TO ME?The ability to posses the tools to develop a quality web application go beyond having the basic skills. A key component of web development is to posses a strong sense of feeling as well as passion and creativity. In essence, coding should be treated as a language - with the use of expression. Developing an app without passion and expression will result in a substandard an unmarketable product. Good web development to me requires dedication and inspired creative interest.
